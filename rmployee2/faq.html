Common Questions

How can I earn points?

Points can be earned through various performance metrics, including completing projects on time, receiving positive feedback, and participating in company events.

What benefits can I get with my points?

Benefits include movie tickets, salary hikes, family tour tickets, and more. Check your dashboard for the latest rewards you are eligible for.

How do I redeem my points?

Log in to your employee dashboard and select the benefit you want to redeem. Your points will be automatically deducted from your total.
